From Data Quality to Trustworthy AI

My research bridges the gap between raw data processing and human-centric decision-making. Starting with a strong foundation in Natural Noise Management and Fuzzy Logic, my current work focuses on the paradigm shift towards Trustworthy Recommender Systems (RS).

Through the FIDELITY project, I address the critical challenges of Fairness and Explainability (XAI) in Group Recommender Systems. I develop agnostic models and fuzzy aggregation techniques to ensure that AI decisions are not only precise but also transparent and equitable. My applied research spans high-impact domains, ranging from nutritional meal planning for health-aware users to e-learning platforms.
